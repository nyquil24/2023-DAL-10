WEED

This is a website to view common types of weeds that you can find growing at home or in the wild.

They are hated by society, but you can't go outside without seeing them.

You can see different species of weed by selecting "Home Grown" or "Wild" from the drop-down.



Facts about Weed

  • Weeds have three life cycles: annual, biennial, and perennial.
  • Weed is a plant
  • Weed is a plant considered undesirable and grows where it is not wanted
  • You shouldn't set them on fire
